A Continued-Series Script Analysis
By: Nikhil Gupta, Jessica Cvetkovska, Jack Detrick
Marvel's Daredevil is a superhero action-drama TV Series that follows Matt Murdock, attorney by day and vigilante by night. Blinded in an accident as a child, Murdock uses his heightened senses as Daredevil to fight crime on the streets of New York after the sun goes down. While Murdock's day job requires him to believe in the criminal justice system, his alter ego does not follow suit, leading him to take the law into his own hands to protect his Hell's Kitchen neighborhood and the surrounding communities. Inspired by the Marvel Comics character, the show first aired on Netflix in April 2015 and continued successfully for 3 seasons into 2018 as a loose tie-in to the larger Marvel Cinematic Universe. However, it was cancelled in November 2018 due to strained relations between Disney and Netflix as Disney+ was soon to launch at the time. Thankfully, the series was revived on Disney+ in March 2025, retitled to Daredevil: Born Again that has run just as successfully for 2 seasons so far with stronger ties to the MCU. This page analyzes both shows in how the story, character interactions, and scripts have changed through all episodes up to s2 x ep5 since it's ongoing at the time of developing the site.
